## Platform Feature Priorities (Inferred)

Based on business analysis, ranked by likely impact:

### Tier 1: Core Needs
- **Programming delivery & athlete tracking** - Write, deliver, track individualized programs (in-person + remote)
- **VBT integration** - Connect VBT devices, use velocity data for autoregulation within programming
- **Client management** - Centralized profiles, intake forms, assessment tracking, communication
- **RPE/RIR and load prescription** - Support percentage-based, RPE-based, and velocity-based load models

### Tier 2: Growth Enablers
- **Template/program marketplace** - Sell programming templates as digital products for passive revenue
- **Competition prep tools** - Attempt selection calculators, warmup timing, weight cut tracking
- **Progress analytics** - Data visualization for client progress (strength gains, volume trends, bodyweight)
- **Group programming** - Manage drop-in days and small group training with shared + individualized programs

### Tier 3: Professionalization
- **Branded client-facing app** - White-label or co-branded mobile experience
- **Scheduling integration** - Native scheduling or Calendly integration
- **Content delivery** - Attach educational content (videos, articles, cues) to exercises
- **Payment processing** - Structured billing for training packages

### Tier 4: Nice-to-Have
- **Exercise video library** - Movement demos attached to programs
- **Injury/rehab tracking** - Track rehab protocols, pain scores, return-to-training progressions
- **Community features** - Leaderboards, group challenges, shared wins for the "Cannoli Gang" community
